What is this blog for?

Good Question! I'm not sure yet. I think it'll just be a place to for me to write things down. Sometimes what I'm working on or what I'm learning. Maybe some family stuff. We'll see.

Why the hell would you quit a job as a Red Sox broadcaster?

Ah the million dollar question. Short answer: I wasn't that happy doing it anymore and I determined that I was likely to become more unhappy as the years progressed. My career is just one facet of my life. It's time for something new.

Ok then, why web development?

I want to build things. I like to figure things out. I've always been very comfortable with technology. There are plenty of things I could be doing, but this is the only thing I came up with that comes close to how I felt about being a brodcaster 20 years ago.

Do you miss broadcasting?

Not that much believe it or not. I don't miss the job. I do miss my friends. I miss hanging around with Joe and Dave. I don't miss working nights and weekends, and I don't miss the travel.
